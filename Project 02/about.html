Sorgen Builders and Development Corporation

SORGEN Builders and Development Corp. Owned and managed by architects and engineers, mostly from the Bicol Region; SORGEN Builders and Development Corp. (SBDC) is a company that supports you on technical and engineering by having our experts in different commodities, produce high quality services, well-managed and in a professional manner.

Our goal is to provide and delivering quality products, competitive, and on-time according to defined specifications, industry standards and compliance. We are dedicated to meet customer expectations in the growing business demand, contribute in gaining access to the emerging wide range markets of our products and latest technology.

 We are aiming to have our partnership develop for many years’ experience between us, by achieved with good communication and understanding, and the correct use of contract management tools reporting, progress evaluation, and an imaginative flexible approach to solving problems.
